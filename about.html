A little more about me

Mission-driven full stack developer with a passion for collaboration and solving challenges.

I've always sought out opportunities and challenges that are meaningful to me. Although my professional path has taken many twists and turns, from park ranger to rock climbing guide to top salesman to small business owner and entrepreneur - I've never stopped engaging my passion to help others and solve problems.

As a web developer, I enjoy using my obsessive attention to detail, my unequivocal love for making things and my mission-driven work ethic to change the world.

I began coding in 2012 while living in Salt Lake City, Utah. My wife, Gina, wanted to create a website to help others who had questions about how to care for rabbits. While helping her create her website, I discovered how fun and challenging it can be to create websites and web applicaitons.
